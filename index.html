Mysore - City of Palace

Mysore is a city of kingdom which is located about 145* Km away from the most populous city of Karnataka i.e. Bangalore. Mysore is noted for its Palaces which includes Mysore Palace which is one of the main attraction of the tourists. Mysore is place of Kings and their Kingdoms. Presently Mysore Palace is owned by a Prince who lives in Bangalore and the Kingdom is maintained by the govt. and is served as a tourist place.
